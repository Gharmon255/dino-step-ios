Dino Step Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026

Dino Step (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) is a step-powered dinosaur hatching game. This policy describes the iOS app and its Apple Watch companion. A separate Android app (with Wear OS companion) is published under the same name with equivalent privacy practices.

Summary

  • We read step count from Apple Health only when you tap Sync Steps.
  • Game progress is stored on your device.
  • We do not sell your data, show ads, or use your steps for tracking across other apps.
  • We do not require an account or collect your email through the app.

Information we access

Steps (Apple Health / HealthKit) — With your permission, the app reads your step count from Apple Health to hatch eggs and grow dinosaurs. Sync happens only when you manually tap Sync Steps on the Home screen. We do not read steps automatically in the background.

We do not access location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.

Information stored on your device

  • Active egg / creature progress (steps, stage, species)
  • Completed creatures and collection stats
  • App preferences (e.g. developer test settings in debug builds only)

This data stays in local app storage unless you uninstall the app or delete app data.

Apple Watch companion

If you install the watchOS companion, your iPhone sends your current creature state (name, stage, progress, art keys) to the watch via Apple’s WatchConnectivity so the watch can display the same egg or dinosaur. This stays within Apple’s ecosystem on your paired devices.

What we do not do

  • No advertising or ad profiling
  • No sale or rental of personal data
  • No cross-app tracking for marketing
  • No server-side account database operated by us for normal gameplay

Children

The app is family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond local game state and optional HealthKit steps on the device.

Data retention and deletion

Uninstalling the app removes local game data. Revoking Health access in Settings stops future step reads; steps already applied to your creature remain in local game state until you reset or uninstall.

Changes

We may update this policy. The effective date at the top will change when we do.
