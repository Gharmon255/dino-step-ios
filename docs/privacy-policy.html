Stepasaurus Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026

Stepasaurus (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) is a step-powered dinosaur hatching game. This policy describes the iOS app and its Apple Watch companion. A separate Android app (with Wear OS companion) is published under the same name with equivalent privacy practices.

Summary

  • We read today’s step count from Apple Health to grow your dinosaur.
  • You can tap Sync Steps on Home anytime; the app may also sync about once per hour via HealthKit background delivery when permission is granted.
  • Game progress is stored on your device by default.
  • Optional account: sign in with Apple or Google to back up game progress or use friend battles. Not required to play.
  • We do not sell your data, show ads, or use your steps for cross-app tracking.

Information we access

Steps (Apple Health / HealthKit) — With your permission, the app reads your step count from Apple Health to hatch eggs and grow dinosaurs. Sync happens when you tap Sync Steps on Home and, with permission granted, about once per hour through HealthKit background delivery. Raw step history is processed on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

We do not access location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.

Information stored on your device

  • Active egg / creature progress (steps, stage, species)
  • Completed creatures and collection stats
  • App preferences

This data stays in local app storage unless you uninstall the app, delete app data, or enable cloud backup (see below).

Optional cloud backup (account)

If you choose Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google in Stats, we store your game save (creature progress, collection, and related stats) on our cloud provider (Supabase) linked to your account identifier and email from the sign-in provider.

  • Step history and raw HealthKit data are not uploaded.
  • Cloud backup is optional; offline play works without an account.
  • You can export a local save file from Stats before signing in.
  • Signing out stops cloud sync; your local save remains on the device.

Optional friend battles (account)

If you sign in and use the Battle tab, we store battle-related data on Supabase linked to your account: challenge codes, species IDs you pick for fights, combat outcomes, and timestamps. Step counts and HealthKit data are not sent for battles.

Notifications

The app may show local notifications on your device for stage milestones and daily step-goal reminders. Notification content is generated on-device; we do not upload notification data to our servers.

Apple Watch companion

If you install the watchOS companion, your iPhone sends your current creature state (name, stage, progress, art keys) to the watch via Apple’s WatchConnectivity so the watch can display the same egg or dinosaur. This stays within Apple’s ecosystem on your paired devices.

What we do not do

  • No advertising or ad profiling
  • No sale or rental of personal data
  • No cross-app tracking for marketing

Children

The app is family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond local game state, optional HealthKit steps on the device, and optional cloud backup or battles if an adult chooses to sign in.

Data retention and deletion

Uninstalling the app removes local game data. Revoking Health access in Settings stops future step reads; steps already applied to your creature remain in local game state until you reset or uninstall. If you used cloud backup or battles, contact us to request deletion of your cloud data; signing out disables further uploads.

Changes

We may update this policy. The effective date at the top will change when we do.
